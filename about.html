THE VOID is a personal blog written by someone who thinks too much about design, spends too much time in terminals, and believes the best ideas come from the quiet hours.

I write about the things I can't stop thinking about: why some interfaces feel alive and others feel dead, how constraints breed creativity, what it means to build something worth keeping.

This blog exists because writing is how I understand things. If something here resonates, I'm glad. I'm writing into the void — but I mean that in the best way possible.
